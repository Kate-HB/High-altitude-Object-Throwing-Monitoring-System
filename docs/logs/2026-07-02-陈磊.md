### 2. 数据库备份与恢复

备份脚本：`scripts/backup-db.ps1`
```powershell
# 备份当前数据库到 data/backup/db_YYYYMMDD_HHMM.sqlite
Copy-Item data/high_altitude_monitor.sqlite data/backup/db_$(Get-Date -Format 'yyyyMMdd_HHmm').sqlite
```

恢复脚本：`scripts/restore-db.ps1`
```powershell
param($backupFile)
Copy-Item $backupFile data/high_altitude_monitor.sqlite -Force
```
